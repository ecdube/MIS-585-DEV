About Us

Drinks

  • Peppermint Mocha (Limited Time) - A sweet and festive drink. Combines Maggie's classic Mocha with peppermint syrup. Ask for the crushed candy cane topping for an extra boost of peppermint!
  • Maggie's Mocha - The signature drink that gave us our name! With our house espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate flavor, there's nothing wrong with a classic.
  • Caramel Macchiato - Steamed milk and espresso, a hint of vanilla and a caramel swirl. Gotta love this sweet drink!
  • Iced Coffee - It's New England, so we have to have an iced coffee. Add a shot of cream for some sweetness!
  • Black Coffee - No muss, no fuss, just a coffee. Sometimes it's like that!

Desserts

  • Peppermint Cookies Limited Time - A small serving of the holiday spirit. These sweet cookies have peppermint flavor baked in and a candy cane topping. Get them in chocolate, white chocolate, or sugar cookie flavors!
  • Peppermint Brownies Limited Time - A slice of the season. These brownies have swirls of peppermint chocolate inside, and come with a candy cane topping. Perfect for a chocolate lover!
  • Muffins - Everyone loves a muffin! Ours come in blueberry, lemon, chocolate, and cinnamon raisin. Warm and fresh out of the oven!
  • Cookies - Our soft, warm cookies hit the spot every time. We have sugar cookies, chocolate chip, double chocolate, white chocolate, and peanut butter.
  • Brownies - Whether you like the center or the edges, our brownies are the best. Get classic chocolate brownies, or try our peanut butter brownies!
  • Croissants - Our warm, flaky croissants are the perfect snack. Try our classic, chocolate, or berry flavors!
